The Proposal

John proposed on a hike to Tamawanas Falls at the base of Mt Hood on a road trip back from spending Christmas in the Bay Area. When we got to the falls, there was another proposal being set up, replete with twinkle lights, a wooden arch, and rose petals scattered on the ground. Not wanting to crash someone else's well-laid plans, John thought "soft pass" and waited until he and Kari were almost back at the car before he pulled her down on the banks of a creek near the trail head and popped the question. As they say, the rest is history.
