Where to Stay

Option A (Choose this one!)

NatureBridge is a working educational camp during the week, which means there are a variety of cabins right at the wedding site! We can fit all of you in and we want you all right next to us the whole time! They are very rustic - think true summer camp. The cabins have twin size bunk beds with camp mattresses and most rooms have one double bed. They sleep 2-6. Linens and towels will be provided. Many cabins share communal bathrooms (which have multiple private showers and stalls). All cabins have electricity and most have heat / AC. Basically - it's glamping. It's glamourous camping. But it's FREE for you!

If you'd like to stay in the cabins (do it do it) please reserve your spot on the RSVP page. Be sure to include your roommate requests and then relax - it's gonna be a little awkward and scary just like camp used to be on the first day. But the good news is this is a wedding so there will be alcohol.

Option B (also totes chill)

For those of you who prefer or require more traditional accommodations we've gathered a few B&B and Hotel options. We're not quite at the high season for Olympic National Park yet, but please do plan in advance as things tend to book quickly. These are the best options currently available, all within a 20-30 minute drive away.

Lake Crescent Lodge Lake Crescent Lodge is conveniently located walking distance from NatureBridge, just next door.
Olympic Lodge Olympic Lodge is a nice hotel located in Port Angeles, about 20-30 minutes from the venue.
National Chains There are also national chains like Red Lion in downtown Port Angeles.
Bed and Breakfasts in Town There are a large number of smaller hotels and B&B's in and around downtown Port Angeles. Some that stood out during our searches were Five Seasuns Bed & Breakfast, A Hidden Haven Forest Cottages, and Rooster Hill Short Stay.

These are the closest and cutest, but there are additional accommodations available, especially if you don't mind a bit of a longer commute to NatureBridge. You can also find a selection of private homes available for rent on AirBnb or VRBO.
