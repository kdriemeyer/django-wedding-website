Gifts

We are blessed to have everything we need -- except your company at our wedding! Please consider spreading the love by donating to a charity of your choice if you feel so moved. If you need a little inspiration, some causes close to our hearts are listed below.

The ACLU

The ACLU of Washington is the Washington State affiliate of the ACLU, which is focused on defending the civil rights of all Americans. We believe that everyone deserves to have their basic rights preserved and protected, and the ACLU is a champion of those who would otherwise be taken advantage of.

Planned Parenthood

Planned Parenthood focuses on preventive health care, informing members of the communities they serve to empower people to make educated choices about their own health. We feel strongly that all people deserve equal access to affordable health care, and also support Planned Parenthood's mission to promote women's rights.

Seattle Children's Uncompensated Care Fund

Seattle Children's provides necessary medical care to all children in the region, regardless of a family's ability to pay. The Uncompensated Care Fund bridges the gap between the cost of care and what a family can afford. Our good friend, Grace, is a resident Audiologist at Seattle Children's and can vouch for the impact that affordable care has for the families served.

Seattle Humane Society

The Seattle Humane Society is where we were first introduced to our fur child, Haines. We're especially fond of the "Sammie's Fund - Older Animals" gift designation, since Haines was 10 when we got him and every elderly animal deserves a loving home.

Washington Trails Association

Hiking has played a big role in our relationship (it was our first date!), and the Washington Trails Association has been a phenomenal resource for trail maintenance, descriptions, and recent conditions. Funding enables the WTA to continue to clear downed trees after storms and fight to keep Washington's natural areas accessible to everyone.
